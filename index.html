Jiarui Fang (方佳瑞)

fang_jiarui AT 163 dot com

I am currently a senior engineer at Wechat AI, Tencent in Beijing. My work includes development of the WeChat input method, the WeChat Open Diague Platform as well as the WeChat Translation System. I am the author of TurboTransformers, a fast runtime for transformer inference on CPU and GPU.

I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tsinghua University at 2019. During my Ph.D., my research focused on High-Performance Computing. Two wonderful experiences outside campus enriched my Ph.D. study. I worked as a research scientist at the National Supercomputing Center in Wuxi from 2016 to 2019. I served as a visiting scholar under supervised of Cho-Jui Hsieh at the University of California, Davis, from 2018 to 2019.
